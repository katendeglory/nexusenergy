Why Hydropower? Hydropower is a proven, efficient, and renewable energy source. Our focus on run-of-river technologies avoids the environmental damage of large dams while delivering clean energy 24/7, even when solar is unavailable.

*Impact*
_Community Impact_
- Electrification of remote villages - Improved access to healthcare (lighting + refrigeration) - Enhanced school attendance and performance - Reduced reliance on kerosene, candles, and diesel generators
_Environmental Impact_
- Run-of-river systems preserve natural flows and fish migration - No large dams, no deforestation - Reduction of CO2 and black carbon emissions
